Luxury Comfort

Famed for being the finest city hotel in Galle, Avendra Hotel is conveniently located in the heart of the Galle city, facing the scenic Indian Ocean and the picturesque Galle Fort. Whether you’re looking to unwind in a luxurious dwelling, indulge in delectable cuisines, or celebrate special moments in life, Avendra Hotel offers an oasis of relaxation and comfort for all. Discover a world of unparalleled elegance and sophistication at Avendra Hotel,where Luxury meets tranquility in the heart of Galle.

Whether you are travelling for business or leisure, we take pride in offering a warm welcome to all our guests with a genuine smile and a commitment to make your stay a memorable one. Our exceptional services and passion for excellence set Avendra Hotel apart from other hotels in Galle. Come and make yourself feel at home — and let us take care of the rest.

Rooms

Offering striking views of the Indian Ocean, Galle Fort and the city beyond, our 500 guest rooms and suites, and 41 serviced apartments are tastefully furnished to complement the urban-oceanside location and are among the largest in Galle.

Spa & Wellness

"You will achieve calmness and profundity, ripeness and wisdom. And the clear enhancement of memory. And most of all, you will have time..." - Lost Horizon by James Hilton

Chi, The Spa at Avendra draws inspiration from the legendary Avendra a place of personal peace, enchantment and well-being. Chi massages and treatments are based on authentic natural healing methods found in traditional well-being practices shared by many Asian cultures. Chi, The Spa at Avendra Hotel Galle features a selection of treatments that showcase Ceylonese natural plants, herbs and botanical products with their potent therapeutic properties. Surrender to the intuitive touch of our skilled therapists within the restorative luxury of Chi, The Spa to revitalize the body and soothe the senses. Seven private rooms are available both for individuals and for couples to experience a sublime selection of traditional Sri Lankan and other Asian treatments and therapies.
