Our Menu

Immersed in the heart of Galle's vibrant dining and entertainment district, Avendra Hotel Galle offers an uncompromising selection of local and international cuisine, al fresco dining, live entertainment, innovative menus and stylish venues to celebrate exquisite culinary flavours.

Vegan Breakfast

Start your day with a delightful vegan breakfast at Avendra Hotel! Our menu features a variety of delicious and wholesome options, including fresh fruit, hearty smoothies, avocado toast, and plant-based pastries. Each dish is thoughtfully prepared using locally sourced ingredients, ensuring a nourishing and satisfying meal to fuel your adventures. Enjoy a tasty and refreshing morning in our serene setting!

Cheese Plate

Indulge in our exquisite cheese plate at Avendra Hotel , featuring a selection of artisanal cheeses paired with fresh fruits, nuts, and gourmet accompaniments. Perfect for sharing or enjoying solo, this delightful offering showcases local flavors and premium quality. Savor the rich and diverse tastes as you relax in our tranquil setting, making it an ideal addition to your dining experience!

Fresh fruits breakfast

Start your day with our fresh fruits breakfast at Avendra Hotel! Enjoy a vibrant selection of seasonal fruits, expertly arranged to showcase their natural flavors. From juicy melons and berries to tropical delights like pineapple and mango, this refreshing option is perfect for a light and nutritious morning meal. Paired with a drizzle of honey or a sprinkle of mint, it’s a delightful way to energize your day!

Ramen

Indulge in our flavorful ramen at Avendra Hotel, featuring rich, savory broth paired with perfectly cooked noodles. Choose from a variety of toppings, including tender vegetables, marinated tofu, and fresh herbs, all expertly crafted to create a delicious bowl that warms the soul. Enjoy this beloved dish in a serene setting, perfect for a comforting meal any time of day!

Rice and Curry

Experience the authentic taste of Sri Lanka with our traditional rice and curry at Avendra Hotel. This delightful meal features fragrant steamed rice served with an array of flavorful curries, including lentils, vegetables, and succulent meats, all cooked with aromatic spices. Accompanied by sides like coconut sambol and crispy papadum, each dish reflects the vibrant culinary heritage of Sri Lanka. Enjoy a truly immersive dining experience that celebrates the rich flavors of the island!

Boiled Chicken

Savor our tender boiled chicken at Avendra Hotel, prepared to perfection for a healthy and delicious meal. This simple yet flavorful dish features succulent chicken gently boiled to retain its natural juices, served with a side of fresh vegetables and a light herb dressing. It’s a wholesome option that’s both nourishing and satisfying, perfect for those seeking a light and protein-rich dining experience!

Vegetable soups

Warm up with our delightful vegetable soups at Avendra Hotel! Made with a medley of fresh, seasonal vegetables and aromatic herbs, each bowl is a nourishing and flavorful experience. Whether you prefer a classic minestrone, a creamy tomato basil, or a hearty vegetable broth, our soups are crafted to highlight the natural goodness of each ingredient. Enjoy a comforting dish that’s perfect for any time of year!

Kiribath / MilkRice

Indulge in our traditional Sri Lankan milk rice at Avendra Hotel! Known as "kiribath," this comforting dish features creamy rice cooked in coconut milk, creating a rich and aromatic flavor. Typically served with a side of spicy sambol or jaggery, it’s a beloved local favorite that’s perfect for breakfast or any meal. Experience the warm, inviting taste of Sri Lankan cuisine with this delightful dish!
